# Boundaries

- This repository is an OmarAGI × NeoMundi technical pilot, not a production deployment.
- It may be described as an early interoperability pilot.
- It may mention a planned or scoped ControlTower live test only where clearly marked as planned or test-scoped.
- It may describe runtime observability signals and REVAS pre-action admissibility artifacts.
- It must not claim official partnership, customer status, production validation, certification, endorsement, or proven cost reduction.
- It must not claim NeoMundi validated OmarAGI.
- It must not claim OpenAI approval, validation, partnership, endorsement, investment, or customer status.
- It must not present synthetic inputs or sanitized artifacts as production records.
- Scores are first-pass REVAS-side routing estimates, not calibrated production metrics.
- NeoMundi behavior signals are not treated as final truth, safety, legal authority, or execution admissibility.
- REVAS-side decisions should preserve uncertainty, route logs, adoption gates, required next steps, and fallback safety.
- Raw confidential materials, private emails, customer data, credentials, internal scratchpads, hidden logs, unapproved raw payloads, and private ControlTower screenshots must not be committed.
